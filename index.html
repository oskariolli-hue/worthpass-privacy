WorthPass — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. General

WorthPass is a life management RPG application. We respect your privacy, and your data belongs to you.

2. Data We Process

WorthPass does not collect or store personal data.

  • Game progress (XP, levels, achievements)
  • User-created tasks (quests)
  • Settings (language, theme)
  • Activity data (streaks)

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may be hosted on platforms like Rosebud AI, which may collect anonymous technical data.

5. Your Rights

You can delete all data at any time from the app or your browser settings.
